Is wealth making our societies safer?

To answer this question we explore how for various countries number of homicides (a proxy for safety) evolved with Per Capita GDP in constant 2010$ terms (a proxy of wealth) using a scatter plot.

Data source is World Development Index parameters.

To see for yourself, you an choose one or more region and click through years to see how the countries in the chosen region has evolved. You can also hover over any of the circle to get more details about a specific country.

Note: Not all countries have homicide data for each year, hence they may appear or disappear as you run through years
